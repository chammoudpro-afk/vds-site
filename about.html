About Valley Data Solutions

Rooted in community. Powered by data. Focused on people.

Our Mission

Valley Data Solutions exists to make data and AI feel less intimidating and more human. We believe small businesses and local communities deserve tools that are understandable, ethical, and genuinely helpful.

How We Work

We listen first, then design solutions that fit your reality. No generic dashboards, no buzzword soup—just thoughtful systems that support your goals.

Community Commitment

Valley Data Solutions is proudly based in Linden, Virginia, nestled near the Shenandoah Valley. Our founder comes from a family of educators, and that background shapes everything we do. We believe schools and students deserve access to the same clarity, empowerment, and modern tools that drive business success.

That’s why we offer our AI Safety & Career Pathways program free to local teachers and classrooms. It’s designed to help students understand what AI is, how to use it responsibly, and how to explore future careers in data, technology, and automation — all with simplicity and real-world relevance.

Whether we’re building dashboards or teaching students about bias and ethics in AI, our mission stays the same: empower communities through clarity and connection.
